Wireless Landmine Detector

Completed this project on course ETE 2200 under the supervision of A. S. M. Badrudduza in 2-2 semester, this project involved detecting landmines wirelessly using advanced sensing technologies.

Components Used:

  • ATmega328 IC
  • L298N H-bridge Dual Motor Driver
  • Metal Detector Module
  • HC-05 Bluetooth Module
  • Crystal Oscillator 16 MHz
  • Capacitors (22 pF, 100 uF, 0.1 uF)
  • Switches
  • 4DCGearMotor
  • LEDs
  • 12Vpower suppl

Outcome: Successfully detected metal objects from a distance of 10 cm.
